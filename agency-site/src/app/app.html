Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before we start.

1. How much time do I need to spend? +

You only need to record content for about 2-3 hours per month. We handle everything else: ideation, scripting, editing, thumbnails, SEO and posting.

2. Do I need to know how to edit videos? +

No. We handle all editing, thumbnails, titles, SEO and publishing. You only focus on recording the content.

3. Who is this service for? +

We work with personal brands, coaches, agencies and business owners who want to grow their brand and get clients through YouTube content.

4. When will I see results? +

Most clients start seeing growth and inbound leads within 30-60 days, depending on their niche, content and consistency.

5. What exactly do you handle? +

We handle the entire content process:

  • Content strategy
  • Video ideas
  • Script writing
  • Video editing
  • Thumbnails
  • Titles & SEO
  • Uploading & posting
6. Do I have to post content myself? +

No, we upload and schedule everything for you.

7. Is there a contract? +

No long-term contracts. We work on a monthly retainer. You can cancel anytime.

8. How much does it cost? +

Pricing depends on the amount of content and services you need. Book a strategy call and we will create a custom plan for you.

9. How do we get started? +

Book a strategy call, we discuss your goals and content strategy, and if it is a good fit we start working together.
